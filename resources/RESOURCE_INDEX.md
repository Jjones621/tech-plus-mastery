## Quality labels

- **Official** — CompTIA-owned source
- **Direct** — specifically targets FC0-U71
- **Legacy** — ITF+ material; map carefully to current objectives
- **Supplemental** — deeper general-IT material
- **Trial/account required** — not fully open without registration

Avoid exam dumps and any source claiming to provide real stolen exam questions.
