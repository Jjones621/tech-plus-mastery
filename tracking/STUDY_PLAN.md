# Six-Week Tech+ Study Plan

## Week 1

- Read the official objectives completely.
- Study tech concepts and terminology.
- Complete 50–100 practice questions.

## Week 2

- Study infrastructure and hardware.
- Complete networking and system-information labs.

## Week 3

- Study applications, operating systems, and software.
- Review weak topics from practice results.

## Week 4

- Study software development, databases, and data concepts.
- Complete spreadsheet, pseudocode, and GitHub labs.

## Week 5

- Study security.
- Complete account, browser, and phishing-awareness labs.

## Week 6

- Take mixed practice tests.
- Revisit every missed objective.
- Build a one-page final review sheet.

Track progress in `tracking/PROGRESS.md`.
