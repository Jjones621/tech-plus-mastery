# Contributing

Add only resources that are:

- Publicly accessible
- Legal to link to and use
- Relevant to FC0-U71 or its underlying concepts
- Clearly labeled as official, direct, legacy, supplemental, or trial/account required

Do not add:

- Exam dumps
- Stolen questions
- Paid-course copies
- Pirated books or videos
- Resources requiring unauthorized access

For each new resource, include its title, URL, category, why it is useful, and any access limitations.
